Our Mission

The Surfing Industry has had a large impact on our Oceans and the Sea life that lives just off our shores. Our goal is to create surf fashion and surfing equipment using sustinable and recycled materials. Such as our driftwoord surfboard range, or our ocean plastics polyester tops. Each purchase you make 20% of that sale will be donated to an Ocean Proctetion and Conservation Charity

Meet Our Founders

Joe Worthington

"Conservation and sustanability have always been a passion of mine, so when me and my partner Hannah decided to set up a surf shack we knew we wanted to make sure we created something sustainable and that would help our world heal."

Hannah Morrison

"There's rarely a weekend you wouldn't find me out on my surf board. From the age of 4 when my Dad first took me out it has always been a passion, surfing the waves and being at one with the sea. Joe and I are both keen on leaving the world a better place, and this is what started Riptide Surf Shack!"
